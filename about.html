ABOUT ME

Ricardo De Guzman

Chess Coach - Player

Hello Chess Lovers,

I'm Chess coach Ricardo with the ranked of International Chess Master.

I was awarded of the IM title by virtue of winning the coveted Asian Junior Chess Championship in the year 1981. Since then it was quite a journey. I am a three-time Olympiad campaigner and was once a Philippine champion.

I also become a part of the after school chess program in the U.S coaching in various schools in the Bay Area, Success Chess Academy, Weibel Chess among others had asked for my coaching services through the years.

I am currently coaching several kids through online class to become future chess Masters. I offered private coaching and group class online. (via Skype, Google Meet, Zoom and other media platform available.)
