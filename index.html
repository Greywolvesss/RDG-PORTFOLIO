What you will learn:

Opening

This chess topic on openings takes the student by the hand, step-by-step, literally explaining the placement of every single piece and making plans in a particular openings set up from both sides.

Middle Game

Lets us improve your middle game. A beginner tries to figure it out the positions and tries to figure out what to do next but falters and missed the best move in such positions.

End Game

Don’t be intimidated by the endgames. With solid endgame understanding you can handles the most complex endings in the various learning techniques you will learned and will begin to learn to love the endings.

Tactics

We cannot calculate every given positions were not a computer machine but we can only do limited moves ahead we must depend on mapping out a good strategy in finding the best plans in a particular positions.

Strategy

Chess strategy includes a wide range of concepts, from knowing how to trade pieces, determining good bishops, bad bishops to evaluating a position. Mastering these principles will greatly enhanced your understanding of chess.

Beginners to Advanced Chess Plays

Learning how to play chess is not always easy, but once you do it can be an amazingly fun game of strategy.
